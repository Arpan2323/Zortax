FAQ

Questions we hear every day

Do you serve both Indian and US clients simultaneously?
+
Yes. Zortax Ledger LLP is specifically structured to handle Indian compliance (GST, ROC, Income Tax) and US accounting & tax work under one roof. Many of our clients are Indian businesses with US operations or NRIs managing assets in both countries.
How do you handle data security for my financial records?
+
We use bank-grade 256-bit encryption for all file transfers, role-based access controls, and signed Non-Disclosure Agreements with every client. Cloud storage is via ISO-27001 certified providers. We never store your data on unencrypted local devices.
Can you take over from my existing accountant mid-year?
+
Absolutely. We handle the transition — collecting prior-period data, reconciling any discrepancies, and bringing everything current. Most transitions are completed within 2 weeks with minimal effort from your side.
What software do you use for US bookkeeping?
+
We are proficient in QuickBooks Online, QuickBooks Desktop, Xero, FreshBooks, and Wave. We can work within your existing software or set up a new account optimised for your business.
Are your fees fixed or hourly?
+
All our engagement plans are fixed-fee with a clearly defined scope of work. You will never receive a surprise invoice. If your requirements exceed the agreed scope, we discuss and agree on any changes before proceeding.
How quickly can you respond to client queries?
+
Standard plans include a 24-hour response SLA. Professional and Comprehensive plan clients receive responses within 4–8 hours. We are available Monday–Saturday and on Indian/US public holidays for urgent matters.

Our team is happy to walk you through your specific situation — no obligation, no sales pitch. Just honest advice from experienced professionals.
